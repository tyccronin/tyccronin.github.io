About Me


Hi there!

My name is Ty Cronin, and I love capturing nature.

I've always been an artist at heart, and started taking up photography as a hobby when I was a young teenager - Borrowing my Dad's camera, and taking pictures of just about anything and everything I could pose for a cool shot. Being such a shy boy, I wasn't much for talking to others or interested in using people as my photo subjects. It was from this little quirk of mine that I turned to nature photography.

Nature is so full of diversity in all aspects, and best of all, I didn't have to talk to it! I've had the pleasure of travelling to a couple different beautiful locations in North America where I found ample subjects to photograph. From the Rocky Mountains, to the majesty of Winter in Winnipeg, I've filled this website with all the best from my portfolio. I'd love nothing more than to inspire fellow hobbiests and artists everywhere with the beauty of nature that I've managed to capture. So please! Stay a while, and enjoy!
