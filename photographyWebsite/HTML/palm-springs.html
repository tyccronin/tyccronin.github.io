Palm Springs, California


On the west coast of the United States, amongst the desert and Rocky Mountain range, there's a little diamond of a place called Palm Springs California. Once a hot spot for celebrities from another age, such as Bob Hope, and Frank Sinatra, now a quieter place where folks go to retire and soak up the sun, and in my case, take some photographs of the unique flowers, landscapes, and nature.
