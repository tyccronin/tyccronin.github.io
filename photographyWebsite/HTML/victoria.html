Victoria, British Columbia


Across a body of water, on Vancouver Island, you'll find the city of Victoria. Once described to me as the city of the newly wed, and the nearly dead, there's no arguing that this Canadian gem is full of life. The colours and lush greenary make these photographs really stand out amongst the rest.
